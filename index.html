About Smart Remove BG

Our Mission

Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Smart Remove BG handles your information when you use our background removal service. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Image Processing

We do not store any images you upload to our service. When you upload an image for background removal:

  • The image is temporarily processed through the remove.bg API to remove the background.
  • Processing occurs in real-time and the image is not saved on our servers.
  • Once processing is complete and you download the result, all temporary data is deleted.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • remove.bg API: For AI-powered background removal. Their privacy policy applies to image processing.
  • EmailJS: For handling contact form submissions. We do not store your contact information.
  • Google AdSense: For displaying non-intrusive ads to support free access to our tool.

Cookies

We may use cookies for:

  • Google AdSense to display relevant advertisements
  • Improving user experience and site functionality
  • Anonymous analytics to understand how our tool is used

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect site functionality.
